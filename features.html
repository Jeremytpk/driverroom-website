Powerful Features for a Secure Fleet

Built to secure your operations, save time, and improve driver morale.

An Indispensable Tool for Every Driver

DriverRoom replaces guesswork and frustration with efficiency and control. Key driver-facing features include:

Verified Gate Code Database

Instant, real-time access to a shared, company-verified database of gate codes, eliminating delays and potential security risks.

Proactive Communication

View daily safety tips and important company announcements directly in the app.

Secure Messaging

Facilitates secure one-on-one and group chats for better fleet communication.

Rescue Management

See detailed assignments for rescue missions to seamlessly support colleagues.

On-Duty Status

Easily check in to a route, signifying on-duty status to the management system.

Comprehensive Controls for Management

Maintain complete oversight of your fleet with powerful, easy-to-use tools. Key management-facing features include:

Total Fleet Management

Track route status, manage driver accounts, and communicate with your entire fleet from one central dashboard.

Analytics and Reporting

Gain actionable insights into route efficiency, driver performance, and common gate-related delays with comprehensive reports.

Role-Based Access Control

Ensure sensitive gate code information is only visible to on-duty drivers and authorized personnel, maintaining a high level of security.
